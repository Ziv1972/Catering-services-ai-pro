# HP Israel Catering Services - Menu Compliance Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-19

**Migrated from:** FoodHouse Analytics

---

## Introduction

This document defines the menu compliance policy for HP Israel catering services across Nes Ziona and Kiryat Gat sites. All menus must comply with these requirements.

## Daily Requirements

### Fresh Salad Bar

Fresh salad bar must be available at every lunch service

**Requirements:**

- Must appear: daily

---

## Dietary

### Daily Vegan Option

A vegan main dish must be available every day

**Requirements:**

- Must appear: daily

---

## Menu Variety

### Fish Day

Fish must be served at least once per week

**Requirements:**

- Must appear: weekly

---

### Schnitzel Limit

Schnitzel should not appear more than twice per week

**Requirements:**

- Maximum frequency: 2 times per week
